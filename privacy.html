This Privacy Policy explains how Bubu Bot (the “Bot”, the “Service”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you invite and use the Bubu Bot Discord bot and when you visit our website at bububot.dev (the “Site”). By using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

Information We Collect

  • Discord IDs and Metadata. We may process user IDs, server (guild) IDs, channel IDs, and role IDs necessary to operate the bot (e.g., game sessions, leaderboards, command permissions).
  • Usernames & Display Names. We may store the username/display name and avatar hash at the time of an action for audit logs and gameplay history.
  • Event & Command Data. When the bot receives events or command usage, we may store timestamps, counters, and configuration choices (e.g., enabled features, selected channels).
  • Content You Provide. We only store the content you intentionally submit to the bot (e.g., command options). We do not read or retain general message content.
  • Logs. We keep server-side logs for reliability and security (e.g., error traces and Discord IDs). Logs are not used for profiling.
  • Website. The Site is hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use first-party cookies or analytics. GitHub or your browser may process standard technical data (IP address, user agent) to serve the Site.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service (e.g., run games, track stats).
  • Enforce rules and prevent abuse, fraud, or misuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.
  • Communicate with server admins (e.g., updates, incidents) via the Support Server.

Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

We rely on legitimate interests to run and secure the Service and on contractual necessity where features are requested by a server. We may also process data to comply with legal obligations.

Data Sharing

  • Discord. We interact with Discord’s APIs to operate the bot.
  • Hosting & Infrastructure. Our hosting provider(s) may process IP addresses and log data to run the Service.
  • Legal/Protection. We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, users, or others, and to detect or address fraud and security issues.
  • No Sale. We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Service and for a reasonable period thereafter for security, auditing, and backup (generally up to 24 months unless a longer period is required by law or operational necessity). We may aggregate or anonymize data for statistics.

Your Choices & Rights

  • Access & Deletion. Server owners/admins can request deletion of server-specific data we control. Users may request removal of their user-specific records where feasible.
  • Opt-Out. Removing the bot from your server will stop new collection for that server. Some records (e.g., security logs) may persist for limited periods.
  • Regional Rights. Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing. We will honor applicable laws.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
